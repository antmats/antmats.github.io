I am a final-year PhD candidate in Machine Learning at Chalmers University of Technology. I am part of the [Healthy AI Lab](https://www.healthyai.se/) led by (Fredrik D. Johansson)[https://www.fredjo.com/]. My research focuses on sequential decision-making with applications in healthcare. I am especially interested in areas such as interpretable policy learning, off-policy evaluation, and domain adaptation.

Graduating in summer 2025, I'm excited to bring my expertise in machine learning and data analysis to an industry role.
